UFC 300: GOAT MMA Card?

Leading up to April 13, fans knew that the next monumental hundred milestone was up and coming. UFC President/CEO, Dana White, made promises of the greatest UFC event of all time. Fans, including I, were skeptical but believed in the hype. Throughout many events, Dana would slowly reveal each fight as he scrambled to assemble the card. The outcome was an amazing card full of fan-favorite and just overall solid fights, arguably one of the best of all time. Check the card information page for some background and details of the fights.
